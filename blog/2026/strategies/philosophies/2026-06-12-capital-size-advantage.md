# Discussion: The Capital Size Advantage (The Retail Edge)

- **Date**: 2026-06-12
- **Participants**: User, Antigravity (Gemini)

## Summary of Discussion

We discussed the "Size Edge"—the structural advantage that smaller investors have over large fund managers (such as Fundsmith or Akre Focus Fund). 

While large funds are restricted to mega-caps because they manage billions of dollars, a smaller investor can invest in micro-cap and small-cap companies. These smaller businesses often have longer growth runways and can compound capital at higher rates. We reviewed Warren Buffett's famous quote where he guaranteed 50% annual returns on $1 million by exploiting this edge.

## Decisions Made
- Created [size_advantage.md](file:///c:/projects/investing/docs/value/size_advantage.md) to document the dynamics of the capital size edge and how to apply quality criteria to smaller firms.
- Agreed to prioritize mid-to-small cap stocks ($100 million to $5 billion market cap) in our screening and research pipelines to exploit this structural edge.
