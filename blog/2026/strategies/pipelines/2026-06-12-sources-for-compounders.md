## Summary of Discussion

We discussed how to practically find high-quality, wide-moat compounders in the real market. 

We mapped out two distinct workflows:
1. **Quantitative Screening**: Using database tools (like TIKR, Koyfin, Finchat) to filter the global stock universe based on hard constraints such as long-term average ROCE/ROIC (>15%), gross margins (>40%), low leverage, and high cash conversion.
2. **Qualitative Tracking (Superinvestor Cloning)**: Using 13F tracking tools (Dataroma, WhaleWisdom) to look inside the portfolios of wide-moat managers like Terry Smith, Chuck Akre, and Nick Sleep to find high-conviction ideas.
