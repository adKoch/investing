- **Date**: 2026-06-12

## Summary of Discussion

We expanded our discussion on quality investing by examining other prominent fund managers who follow the Munger/Buffett philosophy of buying wonderful businesses.

We analyzed the specific guidelines of two modern practitioners:
1. **Terry Smith (Fundsmith)**: Known for his three-step mantra ("Buy good companies. Don't overpay. Do nothing.") and his emphasis on high Return on Capital Employed (ROCE) and Free Cash Flow Yield.
2. **Chuck Akre (Akre Focus Fund)**: Known for his "Three-Legged Stool" framework, which requires a combination of an Exceptional Business, Talented Management, and Great Reinvestment Opportunities to create a "compounding machine."
